Services We Offer

Exterior Detailing, Interior Detailing, Paint Correction, Ceramic Coating, and More!

Exterior Detailing

60 Minutes

Exterior detailing involves a thorough cleaning and restoration of the exterior surfaces of your car. It includes techniques such as washing, waxing, and polishing to remove dirt, grime, and minor imperfections, leaving your vehicle looking clean, glossy, and well-maintained.

Interior Detailing

60 Minutes

Interior detailing focuses on cleaning and rejuvenating the interior of your car. It includes vacuuming, upholstery cleaning, dashboard cleaning, and treating surfaces with conditioners and protectants. Interior detailing helps to restore a fresh and pristine appearance to your car's interior while eliminating odors and enhancing overall comfort.

Book Now

Paint Correction

30 Minutes

Paint correction is a meticulous process of restoring the paintwork of your car to its original condition. It involves removing imperfections like swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation from the paint surface using specialized techniques such as compounding, polishing, and refinishing. Paint correction aims to achieve a flawless and glossy finish, enhancing the overall appearance of your vehicle.

Book Now

Ceramic Coating

45 Minutes

Ceramic coating is a protective layer applied to the exterior surfaces of your car. It creates a strong, durable, and transparent barrier that shields the paint from UV rays, dirt, chemicals, and other contaminants. Ceramic coating provides long-lasting protection, enhances the shine, and makes maintenance easier by repelling water and preventing dirt buildup.
